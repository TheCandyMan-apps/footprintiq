## Crisis Management

### Negative Feedback Protocol

**Step 1: Acknowledge** (Within 1 hour)
- Thank them for feedback
- Show you're listening
- Ask for more details

**Step 2: Investigate** (Within 4 hours)
- Reproduce the issue
- Identify root cause
- Determine fix timeline

**Step 3: Resolve** (Within 24 hours)
- Implement fix
- Update customer
- Offer compensation if needed

**Step 4: Follow Up** (Within 1 week)
- Ensure satisfaction
- Turn into testimonial opportunity

### Common Objections & Responses

**"Too expensive"**
- Compare to using 20 tools separately
- ROI calculation (time saved)
- Free tier available
- Launch discount active

**"Why not use X instead?"**
- Acknowledge competitor
- Highlight unique features
- Offer to help them try both

**"Security concerns"**
- Share security whitepaper
- Explain encryption
- RLS policies
- SOC 2 compliance (in progress)

**"Doesn't work in my country"**
- Check if true
- Roadmap for expansion
- Offer alternative solution
